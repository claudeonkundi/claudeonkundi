About Me

Quality Journalism is vital, especially when documenting collective issues that are directly felt by millions of people. As the world ushers in the decade of action, and various commitments are spearheaded, in achieving the universally accepted, Sustainable Development Goals (SDG's), it is important to document the progress made in addressing the challenges affecting the communities, especially those that have been shown to be at the receiving end, and more vulnerable to climate change. Having documented and produced pieces on sustainable developement including food insecurity, poverty, societal inequalities, global and local policies, and commitments in addressing the existing environmental issues and climate change on digital platforms, I am committed to producing more well researched and compelling news pieces, that will dig deep into Kenya's commitment to the United Nations, and its progress in implementing its Nationally Determined Contributions agreed to at the Paris Climate Agreement.
I am currently a fellow with the Earth Journalism Network.
